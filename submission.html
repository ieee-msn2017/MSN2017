Submission

All papers need to be submitted electronically through the conference website with PDF format. The materials presented in the papers should not be published or under submission elsewhere. Submit a full paper with 5 pages plus up to 2 extra pages (two columns, single-spaced), including figures and references, using 10 fonts, and number each page. You can confirm the Proceedings Author Guidelines at the following web page.

Once accepted, the papers will be submitted for inclusion to the IEEE Computer Society press as well as other Abstracting and Indexing (A&I) database. Authors (at least one) of any accepted paper are requested to register at the conference.
