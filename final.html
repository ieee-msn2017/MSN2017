Final Paper Submission

Since MSN 2017 will be published by Springer, please prepare your manuscript for final camera-ready submission using the Springer templates (see the Springer web page http://www.springer.com/computer/lncs?SGWID=0-164-6-793341-0 for details) Note: The paper not prepared with the template may be rejected! The most common types of papers accepted for publication are full papers (10–15 pages).

Besides, in order to make sure your paper to be published in the conference proceedings, a signed Springer Copyright Form must be submitted for each paper.

Once you are ready, please send both of your final camera-ready paper(include all your source files, doc or latex) and copyright together to wy@dbw.org.cn before December 27th. The title should be “Paper Name+PaperID”.

IMPORTANT: No paper can be published in the proceedings without being accompanied by a Completed Copyright Transfer Form. You must complete and send this form to have your paper included in the conference proceedings.

It is also important to refer the ethics website for all the authors. The website can be found at: https://www.springer.com/gp/authors-editors/journal-author/journal-author-helpdesk/before-you-start/before-you-start/1330#c14214
