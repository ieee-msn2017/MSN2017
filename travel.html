北京理工大学国际交流中心（BIT International Education Communication Center）


地址：北京市海淀区北三环西路66号(近北京理工大学北门)

No. 66, Beisanhuan West Road, Haidian District (near the north gate of BIT).

From Beijing Capital Airport (首都机场)：

    By Taxi: about 34.2KM long around 103RMB (day). It will take 50 minutes if there is no traffic jam.

    By Subway: Take the Airport Subway Line to “Dongzhimen Station” and transfer to Line 2. Then transfer to Line 4 at “Xizhimen Station”. Next, get off at “Renmin University Station (Exit B)”. Finally, walk to the west to arrive at BIT International Education Communication Center.

    乘出租车: 大约34.2公里，大约花费100人民币。如果没有交通堵塞，将需要50分钟。

    乘地铁: 乘坐机场地铁线到“东直门站”换乘地铁2号线，然后在“西直门站”换乘地铁4号线，最后在“人民大学站B口”下车。从人民大学站B口向西步行约1千米到达地点。

From Beijing South Railway Station (北京南站)：

    By Taxi: about 17.4KM long around 60RMB (day). It will take 35 minutes if there is no traffic jam.

    By Subway: Take Line 4 to “Renmin University Station (Exit B)”. Then walk to the west to arrive at BIT International Education Communication Center.

    乘出租车: 大约17.4公里，大约花费60人民币。如果没有交通堵塞，将需要35分钟。

    乘地铁: 乘坐地铁4号线到“人民大学站B口”下车。从人民大学站B口向西步行约1千米到达地点。

From Beijing West Railway Station (北京西站)：

    By Taxi: about 9.2KM. long around 25RMB (day). It will take 20 minutes if there is no traffic jam.

    By Subway: Take Line 9 to "National Library Station" to transfer to line 4, and get off at "Renmin University Station (Exit B)". Then, walk to the west to arrive at BIT International Education Communication Center.

    乘出租车: 大约9.2公里，大约花费25人民币。如果没有交通堵塞，将需要20分钟。

    乘地铁: 乘坐地铁9号线到“图书馆站”换乘地铁4号线，在“人民大学站B口”下车。从人民大学站B口向西步行约1千米到达地点。

You can show the below information to taxi driver:

请带我去北京理工大学国际交流中心，它在北理工的北门。谢谢您！
